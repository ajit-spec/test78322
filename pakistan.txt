Pakistan is a country in South Asia, bordering India, Afghanistan, Iran and China.
It has a rich history spanning ancient civilizations and is known for its diverse landscapes from mountains to deserts.
The capital city of Pakistan is Islamabad, which serves as the political, economic, and cultural center of the country.
Pakistan is also known for its diverse cuisine, including dishes like biryani, kebab, and naan bread.
The country has a population of over 220 million people, making it the world's fifth-most populous nation.
Pakistan is also famous for its rich culture, including music, dance, and literature.
